# HabitLoop

HabitLoop is a simple and intuitive **habit tracking app** designed to help you build and maintain good daily habits.  

With HabitLoop, you can:  
- Add new habits with a **title** and **difficulty level** (Easy, Medium, Hard)  
- Track your progress daily with checkboxes and progress indicators  
- Visualize your habit streaks and stay motivated  

## Features
- **Add habits** quickly and easily  
- **Track progress** with daily checkboxes  
- **Progress indicators** to see your habit streak  
- **Clean and simple UI** with Jetpack Compose
